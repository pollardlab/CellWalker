Low, medium, or high within-type distinctness data generated by splitting bins evenly across cell types and adding a fixed percent (1, 5 or 10 respectively) 
are in in Distinctness.zip

Data with an additional cells made more similar to one of the previous cell types by being generated by randomly sampling reads proportionally to bins from either 
of the other two cell types, with the proportion of bins from the cell type adjusted between 10 and 50 percent are in Skew.zip
